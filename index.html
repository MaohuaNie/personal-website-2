About Me

I am a PhD student researching decision-making processes, focusing on the psychology behind economic behavior. My work explores how individuals make choices under risk and the influence of complexity on decision-making.

Research Interests

My research interests are mainly decision making under risk, especially under the effect of different factors like choice complexity.
